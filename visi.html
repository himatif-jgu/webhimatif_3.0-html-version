Who Are We Looking For?

  • You are : An Informatics Engineering Student
  • Enthusiasm : Curious & willing to learn
  • Collaboration : Enjoy working in a team
  • Contribution : Eager to share ideas and thoughts
  • Integrity : Uphold the good name of the alma mater

Our Vision Himatif

Realizing HIMATIF as a student association that is collaborative, innovative, interactive, and embraces each other, and can create a comfortable, active, solutive, and responsive environment for Informatics Engineering students.

Work Program

  • Organizing collaborative and inclusive activities to strengthen the relationship among all Informatics Engineering students.
  • Organizing training and workshops to improve the technical and non-technical skills of members.
  • Building cooperation networks with other student associations, industries, and professional organizations.
  • Managing an effective and transparent communication system for disseminating information to all members.
  • Initiating social awareness-based activities to address current issues in society through technology.
  • Completing and realizing planned programs responsibly.

Benefits for Members

  • Skill Development: Gain access to exclusive training and workshops to improve industry-relevant hard skills and soft skills.
  • Extensive Network: Build strong relationships with fellow students, seniors, alumni, lecturers, and industry partners.
  • Career Opportunities: Connect with the professional world earlier through company visit programs, career seminars, and information on internship/job vacancies.
  • Innovation Hub: Get a supportive environment to channel ideas, collaborate on projects, and build a portfolio.
  • Organizational Experience: Learn leadership, project management, and teamwork, which are essential added values beyond lectures.
